My Process

The Roadmap to Flawless Deliverables

A transparent, stage-by-stage workflow ensuring your design intent is perfectly preserved.

Stage 01

Design Discovery & Plans

We align on your spatial concepts. You supply the CAD drawings, hand-sketched layouts, reference boards, or BIM files. We establish material choices, environment context, and final shot lists.

Stage 02

3D Clay Modeling

I construct the spatial structure inside our drafting graphics suite. You receive clean "clay model" renders showing layout grids, scale proportions, and camera angles. This locks down geometry.

Stage 03

Materializing & Lighting

We apply rich textures—from wood grain orientation to brick joints—and configure real-world physical light patterns (sun directions, atmospheric scattering, internal fixtures). You approve these frames.

Stage 04

High-Fidelity Rendering

Using advanced raytracing graphics farms, we compile the final images in ultra HD resolution or compile the high-frame-rate, high-bitrate cinematic walk-through video tracks.

Stage 05

Cinematic Delivery

Color grading, environment details (human scales, surrounding foliage), and post-production are applied. You receive premium, high-resolution renders and market-ready cinematic tracks.

FAQ

Common Questions

Answering architectural project concerns upfront to build immediate trust.

I work with a wide range of input types to accommodate you. You can supply 2D CAD layouts (.dwg, .dxf), fully constructed 3D models (.obj, .skp, .fbx, .rvt), hand-sketched blueprints with annotations, or even detailed material specification lists. The more details you share, the faster we lock down clay modeling.

I provide 2 comprehensive rounds of revisions at key milestones (Stage 02 Clay Modeling and Stage 03 Lighting & Material mapping) to guarantee details match your plans exactly. Revision loops are conducted transparently to ensure adjustments are locked in before high-res raytracing.

A standard interior or exterior render takes roughly 5 to 7 business days, depending on project scale complexity. High-end cinematic animations require approximately 2 to 3 weeks due to camera mapping and computational frame rendering. We establish hard milestone deliverables inside our initial onboarding agreements.

Final rendering images are delivered as ultra-high-resolution files (4K UHD) in lossless formats (PNG/TIFF) optimized for full-scale banner printing or investor slide decks. Animations are exported in smooth 1080p/4K 60FPS digital streams suitable for corporate screens, social feeds, or pre-sales marketing website integrations.
